## Welcome to Mascotas

This guide will help you get started with Mascotas, whether you need to report a lost pet, have found a pet, or are looking to adopt. Follow these steps to set up your account and start using the platform.

<Note>
  Mascotas is designed for the Mar del Plata community. Make sure you're located in or near Mar del Plata to get the most relevant results.
</Note>

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit the Mascotas platform and click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.
    
    You can create an account using:
    - Email and password
    - Google account (recommended for faster setup)
    
    <Tip>
      Using Google sign-in allows for quicker account creation and easier password recovery.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email">
    If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification email from Mascotas. Click the verification link to activate your account.
    
    <Info>
      The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new verification email from the login page.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After logging in, complete your profile with:
    
    - **Full name**: This helps build trust with other users
    - **Phone number**: Required for WhatsApp notifications
    - **Location**: Select your neighborhood in Mar del Plata (used for location-based matching)
    - **Profile photo** (optional): Adds credibility to your reports
    
    <Warning>
      Your phone number is required to receive WhatsApp notifications about potential pet matches. We never share your phone number publicly.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set notification preferences">
    Configure how you want to be notified:
    
    - **WhatsApp notifications**: Get instant alerts (recommended)
    - **Email notifications**: Daily digest or real-time alerts
    - **Notification radius**: Set how far from your location you want to receive alerts (1-20 km)
    - **Pet types**: Choose which types of pets you want notifications for (dogs, cats, birds, etc.)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Report a lost pet

If your pet is missing, create a lost pet report immediately to alert the community.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Report Lost Pet'">
    From the dashboard, click the **Report Lost Pet** button or navigate to **Reports** > **Lost Pets** > **New Report**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add pet details">
    Fill in the following information:
    
    - **Pet name**: Your pet's name
    - **Pet type**: Dog, cat, bird, rabbit, etc.
    - **Breed**: Specific breed or mix
    - **Size**: Small, medium, large, extra large
    - **Color and markings**: Detailed description of fur color, patterns, and unique markings
    - **Age**: Approximate age
    - **Gender**: Male, female, or unknown
    - **Photos**: Upload 2-5 clear photos (required)
    
    <Tip>
      Include photos from multiple angles. Close-ups of unique markings or distinctive features help with identification.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify location">
    - **Last seen location**: Click on the map or enter the address where your pet was last seen
    - **Date and time**: When you last saw your pet
    - **Search radius**: How far from the last seen location you want to search (default: 5 km)
    
    <Info>
      The more precise the location, the better. If your pet went missing during a walk, mark the exact spot where they ran off.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add additional information">
    Include any relevant details:
    
    - Medical conditions or medications
    - Collar information (color, tags, ID number)
    - Microchip number (if applicable)
    - Behavioral traits (shy, friendly, scared of loud noises, etc.)
    - Reward information (optional)
    
    <Warning>
      Never include sensitive personal information like your home address in the public description. Use the platform's messaging system for private communication.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and publish">
    Review all information for accuracy, then click **Publish Report**. Your report will be:
    
    - Visible on the lost pets map
    - Searchable by other users
    - Matched against found pet reports
    - Sent to users who have enabled notifications in your area
    
    You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to manage your report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After publishing, you can edit your report at any time from your dashboard. If your pet is found, mark the report as **Resolved** to notify the community.
</Note>

### Report a found pet

If you've found a pet, create a found pet report to help reunite them with their owner.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Report Found Pet'">
    From the dashboard, click **Report Found Pet** or navigate to **Reports** > **Found Pets** > **New Report**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Document the pet">
    Provide as much detail as possible:
    
    - **Pet type and breed**: Best guess if you're not sure
    - **Physical description**: Size, color, markings, approximate age
    - **Photos**: Upload clear photos showing the pet's face and any distinctive features
    - **Condition**: Note if the pet appears injured, scared, well-groomed, or wearing a collar
    
    <Tip>
      Take photos of any collar tags, even if you can't read them clearly. Owners may recognize their pet's collar.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify where you found them">
    - **Found location**: Mark the exact location on the map
    - **Date and time**: When you found the pet
    - **Current location**: Where the pet is now (if different from where you found them)
    
    <Info>
      If you've taken the pet to a shelter or vet, include that information in the current location field.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your contact method">
    Choose how people can reach you:
    
    - In-app messaging (always available)
    - WhatsApp (recommended for faster response)
    - Phone call
    
    <Warning>
      Be cautious when meeting strangers. Always meet in public places and consider bringing a friend. Ask potential owners to describe distinctive features before meeting.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish the report">
    Click **Publish Report**. The system will:
    
    - Automatically match your report against lost pet reports
    - Notify potential owners who match the description and location
    - Display the found pet on the map
    
    You'll receive notifications when someone reaches out about the pet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Search for matches

Browse existing reports to find potential matches for lost or found pets.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the map view">
    Navigate to **Map** to see all lost and found pet reports in Mar del Plata.
    
    - **Filter by type**: Lost pets, found pets, or adoption listings
    - **Filter by species**: Dogs, cats, birds, etc.
    - **Adjust radius**: Zoom in/out to focus on specific areas
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the search function">
    Go to **Search** and use filters:
    
    - Pet type and breed
    - Color and size
    - Date range (when lost/found)
    - Location (neighborhood or specific area)
    
    <Tip>
      Start with broad search criteria, then narrow down. A pet's appearance may be described differently by different people.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review potential matches">
    Click on any report to view full details:
    
    - Compare photos carefully
    - Check location and dates
    - Read the full description
    - Look for distinctive markings or features
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact the poster">
    If you find a potential match:
    
    1. Click **Contact Owner** or **Contact Finder**
    2. Send a message through the platform
    3. Describe why you think it's a match
    4. Ask verification questions (e.g., "Does your dog have a scar on the left ear?")
    
    See our full guide: [Contact owners and finders](/guide/contact-owners)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How quickly will I get notifications about potential matches?">
    Notifications are sent in real-time when a new report matches your criteria. WhatsApp notifications are typically delivered within seconds, while email notifications may take a few minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I report a pet on behalf of someone else?">
    Yes, but include the actual owner's contact information in the report so potential finders can reach them directly. Make sure you have their permission first.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I find my lost pet outside the platform?">
    Great news! Log in to your dashboard, find your lost pet report, and click **Mark as Resolved**. This removes your report from active searches and notifies anyone who may have been following your case.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a cost to use Mascotas?">
    No, Mascotas is completely free to use. Our mission is to help every pet in Mar del Plata find their way home.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I verify someone claiming to have found my pet?">
    Always ask specific questions that only the true owner would know, such as distinctive markings, behavioral quirks, or medical conditions. Meet in a public place and bring a friend if possible.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
