## Getting Started with Mascotas

Creating an account on Mascotas allows you to report lost or found pets, list pets for adoption, and connect with other pet owners in Mar del Plata.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner of the homepage. You'll be redirected to the registration page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Sign-Up Method">
    You can create an account using:
    - **Email and Password**: Enter your email address and create a strong password
    - **Google Account**: Click "Continue with Google" for faster registration
    - **Facebook Account**: Click "Continue with Facebook" to use your existing profile

    <Tip>
      Using Google or Facebook sign-up is faster and eliminates the need to remember another password.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    If you signed up with email and password, check your inbox for a verification email from Mascotas. Click the verification link to activate your account.

    <Note>
      The verification link expires after 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one from the login page.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    After verification, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:
    - **Full Name**: Your first and last name
    - **Phone Number**: Used for WhatsApp contact (optional but recommended)
    - **Location**: Select your neighborhood in Mar del Plata
    - **Profile Photo**: Upload a clear photo (optional)

    <Warning>
      Your phone number will be visible to other users when you post about pets. Only provide a number you're comfortable sharing.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Preferences">
    Configure your notification preferences:
    - Email notifications for matches to your lost pet reports
    - SMS alerts for found pets in your area
    - Weekly digest of pets available for adoption

    You can change these settings anytime from your account settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Settings

After creating your account, you can access your profile settings by clicking your profile icon in the top right corner and selecting **Settings**.

### Important Profile Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact Information" icon="phone">
    Keep your phone number and email updated so pet owners can reach you quickly.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Privacy Settings" icon="lock">
    Control who can see your profile and contact information.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Notification Preferences" icon="bell">
    Customize how and when you receive alerts about pets.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Location Settings" icon="map-marker">
    Update your neighborhood to see relevant pet reports nearby.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I didn't receive the verification email">
  Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still can't find it:
  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Resend verification email"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your inbox again (it may take a few minutes)
  
  If the problem persists, contact support@mascotas.com
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I forgot my password">
  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for the password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new password
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my email address?">
  Yes, you can change your email address in Settings:
  1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
  2. Click **Change Email**
  3. Enter your new email address
  4. Verify the new email by clicking the link sent to it
  
  <Note>
    You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
  To permanently delete your account:
  1. Go to **Settings** > **Account**
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click **Delete Account**
  3. Confirm your decision
  
  <Warning>
    Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your posts and data will be removed.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>
