## Report a Found Pet

If you've found a pet in Mar del Plata, reporting it on Mascotas can help reunite them with their owner quickly. Follow this guide to create an effective found pet report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Report Form">
    From the main dashboard, click the **Report Found Pet** button. You can also navigate to **Reports** > **Found Pet** from the menu.

    <Info>
      You don't need an account to view found pet reports, but you must be logged in to create one.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Take Clear Photos">
    If possible, take 3-5 clear photos of the pet:
    - Face and front view
    - Side profile showing body and tail
    - Any distinctive markings, collar, or tags
    - Any injuries or unique features

    <Tip>
      Approach the pet carefully. If they seem frightened or aggressive, maintain a safe distance and try to photograph them from afar. Your safety comes first.
    </Tip>

    <Warning>
      If the pet appears injured or in distress, contact local animal control or a veterinary clinic immediately before creating a report.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe the Pet">
    Provide as much detail as possible:
    
    **Physical Description**
    - Species (dog, cat, or other)
    - Approximate breed or mix
    - Size (small, medium, large)
    - Age estimate (puppy/kitten, young, adult, senior)
    - Gender (if known)
    - Colors and patterns

    **Identifying Features**
    - Collar description and any visible tags
    - Unique markings or scars
    - Missing teeth or unusual features
    - Microchip (if you had them scanned at a vet)

    <Note>
      Don't worry if you're unsure about some details. Any information helps, and owners will recognize their pets from photos and distinctive features.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Location Details">
    Specify where you found the pet:
    - **Location Found**: Click on the map or enter the street address
    - **Date and Time Found**: When you first saw or secured the pet
    - **Neighborhood**: Select the area from the dropdown
    - **Current Location**: If you've taken the pet to a different location (your home, vet, shelter), specify this

    <Info>
      The platform will automatically match your report with nearby lost pet reports and notify potential owners.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe Pet Behavior and Condition">
    Provide information about the pet's state:
    - Temperament (friendly, scared, aggressive)
    - Health condition (healthy, injured, malnourished)
    - Whether they're wearing a collar
    - If they seem lost or stray
    - Any immediate needs (food, water, medical attention)

    <Tip>
      Mention if the pet seems well-cared-for (groomed, healthy weight, wearing collar) versus appearing to be a stray. This helps identify if they're truly lost.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Contact Information">
    Provide how people can reach you:
    - Phone number (for WhatsApp contact)
    - Email address
    - Best times to contact you
    - Your availability to help with reunification

    <Note>
      If you've temporarily housed the pet, mention your availability for the owner to pick them up.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify Current Pet Custody">
    Indicate where the pet is now:
    - **In Your Care**: You're temporarily housing the pet
    - **At a Shelter**: You've taken them to a local animal shelter (include name and address)
    - **At a Vet**: They're receiving medical care (include clinic name)
    - **Still in Area**: The pet is still roaming but you've seen them multiple times

    <Warning>
      If the pet is injured, take them to a veterinarian or animal shelter before creating the report. Their immediate health is the priority.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Publish">
    Review all information before publishing:
    1. Double-check photos are clear and show important features
    2. Verify location and contact details are accurate
    3. Check the box confirming the information is truthful
    4. Click **Publish Report**

    Your report will immediately appear on the platform and map, and the system will notify owners of matching lost pets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Caring for a Found Pet

<Accordion title="Immediate care steps">
  If you're temporarily housing a found pet:
  
  **Safety First**
  - Keep them separate from your own pets until health status is confirmed
  - Check for injuries or signs of illness
  - Provide fresh water immediately
  
  **Basic Needs**
  - Offer food (start with small amounts)
  - Create a safe, quiet space for them to rest
  - Keep them indoors if possible to prevent them from running away again
  
  **Health Check**
  - Take them to a vet to check for microchip
  - Get any urgent medical issues addressed
  - Keep records of any expenses for potential reimbursement
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verifying the true owner">
  When someone contacts you claiming to be the owner:
  
  **Ask for Verification**
  - Request photos of them with the pet
  - Ask them to describe distinctive features not mentioned in your report
  - Check if they have veterinary records or microchip information
  - Ask for the pet's name and see if the pet responds
  
  **Red Flags**
  - Unwillingness to provide verification
  - Descriptions that don't match the pet
  - Suspicious behavior or rushing the process
  
  <Warning>
    If something feels wrong, trust your instincts. You can ask to meet at a public location or involve a local shelter or veterinarian in the reunification.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="If no owner is found">
  If several weeks pass with no owner claiming the pet:
  
  **Next Steps**
  1. Continue keeping the report active
  2. Contact local shelters to see if anyone has reported them missing through other channels
  3. Have a vet check for microchip again
  4. Check local lost pet social media groups
  
  **Long-term Options**
  - Consider adopting the pet yourself
  - Work with a local rescue organization to find them a home
  - List them for adoption on Mascotas (update the report type)
  
  <Info>
    In Argentina, there are specific laws about found animals. After a reasonable search period (typically 30-60 days), you may be able to legally adopt or rehome the pet.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if the pet runs away again?">
  If the found pet escapes before being reunited:
  
  1. Update your report immediately with the new location
  2. Add a note that the pet ran away and specify when/where
  3. Keep checking the area at different times of day
  4. Leave food and water in the area where they were last seen
  5. Alert neighbors in the new area
  
  Don't feel discouraged - this is common with frightened lost pets.
</Accordion>

## Managing Your Report

### Updating Information

To edit your found pet report:
1. Go to **My Reports** in your dashboard
2. Select the found pet report
3. Click **Edit**
4. Update any changed information
5. Click **Save Changes**

<Tip>
  If you learn new information about the pet (name from tag, microchip number, health issues), update the report immediately.
</Tip>

### Marking as Reunited

When the pet has been returned to their owner:
1. Go to your report
2. Click **Mark as Reunited**
3. Optionally share a success story
4. Confirm the action

This will:
- Close the active report
- Notify users who were tracking the report
- Add the success story to the community feed
- Keep the report in your history

<Info>
  Success stories inspire the community and show the platform's effectiveness in reuniting pets with their families.
</Info>
