## Report Your Lost Pet

Time is critical when a pet goes missing. Follow this guide to create an effective lost pet report that will help the Mar del Plata community assist in finding your pet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Report Form">
    From the main dashboard, click the **Report Lost Pet** button. You can also access this from the navigation menu under **Reports** > **Lost Pet**.

    <Note>
      You must be logged in to create a lost pet report.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Clear Photos">
    Upload 3-5 clear, recent photos of your pet:
    - Front view showing face and markings
    - Side profile
    - Any distinctive features (scars, spots, unique markings)

    <Tip>
      High-quality photos significantly increase the chances of your pet being recognized. Use well-lit photos that clearly show your pet's features.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Pet Details">
    Provide detailed information about your pet:
    
    **Basic Information**
    - Pet name
    - Species (dog, cat, or other)
    - Breed (or mix)
    - Age
    - Gender
    - Size (small, medium, large)
    - Primary and secondary colors

    **Distinctive Features**
    - Unique markings or patterns
    - Scars or injuries
    - Missing teeth or other physical characteristics
    - Collar description (color, tags)

    <Warning>
      Include as many distinctive features as possible. These details help distinguish your pet from similar-looking animals.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Specify Location and Time">
    Provide accurate location details:
    - **Last Seen Location**: Click on the map or enter the address where your pet was last seen
    - **Date and Time**: When you last saw your pet
    - **Neighborhood**: Select your neighborhood from the dropdown
    - **Search Radius**: Set how far from the location you want alerts (500m, 1km, 2km, 5km)

    <Info>
      The platform will automatically notify users in the selected area who have reported finding a pet matching your description.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Behavioral Information">
    Describe your pet's behavior and temperament:
    - Friendly or shy with strangers
    - Response to their name
    - Favorite treats or toys
    - Any medical conditions or special needs
    - Whether they're microchipped

    <Tip>
      Behavioral information helps people approach your pet safely and increases the likelihood they'll respond positively.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Contact Preferences">
    Choose how people can contact you:
    - Phone number (for WhatsApp contact)
    - Email address
    - Preferred contact method
    - Best times to reach you

    Your contact information will be visible to verified users who click "Contact Owner" on your report.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Publish">
    Review all the information carefully before publishing. Once satisfied:
    1. Check the "I confirm this information is accurate" box
    2. Click **Publish Report**

    Your report will be immediately visible to the community and appear on the map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Publishing Your Report

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Monitor Matches" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Check your dashboard regularly for potential matches with found pet reports in your area.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Respond Quickly" icon="clock">
    Respond to messages and notifications promptly. Quick communication increases reunion chances.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Update Information" icon="pen">
    If your pet is sighted in a new location, update the report with the new information.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Mark as Found" icon="check">
    When you find your pet, mark the report as "Found" to stop receiving notifications and notify the community.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Maximizing Your Search Efforts

<Accordion title="Share your report on social media">
  After publishing, click the **Share** button on your report to:
  - Share on Facebook
  - Share on Twitter
  - Share on Instagram Stories
  - Copy a direct link to your report
  
  The more people who see your report, the better your chances of finding your pet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Expand your search radius">
  If your pet hasn't been found after 24-48 hours:
  1. Go to your report
  2. Click **Edit Report**
  3. Increase the search radius to 5km or more
  4. Save changes
  
  Pets can travel farther than expected, especially if they're frightened.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Print physical flyers">
  Click **Download Flyer** on your report to generate a printable PDF with:
  - Your pet's photo
  - Key details and description
  - Contact information
  - QR code linking to the online report
  
  Post these in your neighborhood, veterinary clinics, pet stores, and community centers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Contact local shelters and vets">
  While Mascotas reaches many people, also contact:
  - Local animal shelters in Mar del Plata
  - Veterinary clinics in the area
  - Pet stores and grooming salons
  - Animal control services
  
  Provide them with your report link so they can easily identify your pet.
</Accordion>

## Editing or Closing Your Report

### Updating Information

To edit your report:
1. Go to **My Reports** in your dashboard
2. Find the lost pet report
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make your changes
5. Click **Save Changes**

<Tip>
  If there's a confirmed sighting in a new location, update the "Last Seen Location" immediately to focus the search area.
</Tip>

### Marking as Found

When you've reunited with your pet:
1. Go to your report
2. Click **Mark as Found**
3. Optionally, share a happy reunion message
4. Confirm the action

This will:
- Stop all active notifications
- Update the report status
- Notify users who have been helping with the search
- Remove the report from active searches

<Info>
  Found reports remain visible in your profile history but are no longer shown in active searches.
</Info>
