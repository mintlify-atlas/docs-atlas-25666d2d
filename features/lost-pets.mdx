The Lost Pets feature helps you quickly mobilize the community when your pet goes missing. Create detailed reports with photos, location data, and real-time notifications to maximize the chances of a safe reunion.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Lost Pet Report">
    Navigate to the dashboard and click "Report Lost Pet". Fill in essential details:
    - Pet's name, species, breed, and age
    - Physical description and distinguishing features
    - Upload clear, recent photos (up to 5)
    - Last seen location with exact address or map pin
    - Date and time last seen
    - Your contact preferences (phone, WhatsApp, email)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Search Radius">
    Define the geographic area for your search. The system will:
    - Notify users within your selected radius
    - Display your report on the map for nearby users
    - Send alerts to anyone who reported found pets in the area
    - Automatically expand the radius after 24 hours (optional)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Match Notifications">
    Get instant alerts when:
    - Someone reports finding a pet matching your description
    - A user spots your pet and submits a sighting
    - Relevant found pet reports are posted nearby
    - Community members comment with tips or sightings
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update and Manage Your Report">
    Keep your report current:
    - Add new sightings with locations and timestamps
    - Update the search radius as needed
    - Share your report link on social media
    - Mark as "Found" when reunited
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Act Fast**: Create your report within the first 24 hours. Pets are most often found within 1-2 miles of where they went missing during the first 48 hours.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Photo Quality Matters**: Use clear, well-lit photos showing your pet's face and any distinctive markings. Include photos from multiple angles.
</Note>

<Info>
  **Update Regularly**: Even if you have no new information, update your report every 2-3 days. This keeps it active and visible to new community members.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Privacy Settings**: Choose your contact visibility carefully. You can hide your full name and address while still allowing people to reach you through the platform.
</Warning>

## Report Details

### Required Information

To create an effective lost pet report, include:

- **Photos**: At least 2 clear photos (5 recommended)
- **Physical Description**: Color, size, weight, breed specifics
- **Distinguishing Features**: Scars, unique markings, collar details
- **Last Known Location**: Specific address or GPS coordinates
- **Date/Time**: When your pet was last seen
- **Contact Method**: At least one way for finders to reach you

### Optional but Helpful

- Microchip number and registry
- Pet's temperament (friendly, shy, aggressive)
- Medical conditions or special needs
- Reward information (if offering)
- Recent behavioral changes
- Favorite treats or commands they respond to

## Managing Your Search

### Search Radius Options

Choose the appropriate radius for your area:

- **500m - 1km**: Dense urban areas, apartment buildings
- **2-5km**: Suburban neighborhoods
- **10km+**: Rural areas or if pet has been missing 3+ days

### Sighting Reports

When someone reports a sighting:

1. You receive an instant notification with the exact location
2. The sighting appears on your report's map
3. You can contact the reporter directly through the platform
4. Add the sighting to your timeline to track movement patterns

### Ending Your Search

When you find your pet:

1. Click "Mark as Found" on your report
2. Optionally share your reunion story to inspire others
3. Thank community members who helped
4. Your report moves to the "Success Stories" archive

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How long does my report stay active?">
  Lost pet reports remain active indefinitely until you mark them as found or closed. However, we recommend updating your report every few days to keep it visible in community feeds. Reports without updates for 30 days will be flagged for your attention.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I edit my report after posting?">
  Yes, you can edit all details of your report at any time. Click the "Edit Report" button on your dashboard. Changes are saved immediately and notifications are sent to followers if you update critical information like location or description.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if someone finds my pet?">
  When someone reports finding a pet matching your description, you'll receive an immediate notification via your preferred method (WhatsApp, SMS, email, or push). You'll see their contact information and the exact location where your pet was found. You can then coordinate the reunion directly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does the matching algorithm work?">
  Our system compares your lost pet report with all found pet submissions in your area. It analyzes species, breed, color, size, distinctive markings, and location proximity. When confidence is high (70%+), both parties receive a match notification with contact details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I share my report outside the platform?">
  Absolutely! Each report has a unique shareable link that displays all your pet's information and photos. Share this link on social media, community groups, or with local veterinarians and shelters. The link works for anyone, even without a Mascotas account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a cost to report a lost pet?">
  Creating and managing lost pet reports is completely free. All features including notifications, map integration, and matching are included at no cost. Our mission is to reunite pets with their families.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I receive a false match notification?">
  If a match notification isn't your pet, simply dismiss it. You can also mark it as "Not a Match" which helps improve our algorithm. If you receive too many false matches, try adding more specific details or better photos to your report.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I have multiple active reports?">
  Yes, you can have multiple active reports if you have several pets missing. Each report is managed independently with its own notifications, map, and timeline. Access all your reports from your dashboard.
</Accordion>

## Success Tips

### Within the First Hour

- Create your report immediately with any photos you have
- Set a 2-5km search radius
- Walk the immediate area calling your pet's name
- Check common hiding spots (under porches, in garages, etc.)

### First 24 Hours

- Update your report with any new information
- Share your report link in local Facebook groups
- Contact nearby veterinary clinics and shelters
- Put up physical posters in the area (include your report QR code)

### Days 2-7

- Expand your search radius by 50%
- Post updates to keep your report visible
- Check the found pets map daily for new matches
- Visit local animal shelters in person

### After One Week

- Continue regular updates
- Consider expanding your radius to 10km+
- Re-share on social media
- Don't give up - pets are often found weeks or months later
