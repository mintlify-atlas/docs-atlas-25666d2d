## For Lost Pets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pin Last Known Location">
    When creating a lost pet report:
    - Search for the address or use current location
    - Drop a pin exactly where your pet was last seen
    - Add location notes ("near the blue gate", "by the dog park entrance")
    - The system records GPS coordinates and timestamp
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Search Radius">
    Define the alert zone:
    - Start with 1-5km depending on your area type
    - Urban areas: smaller radius (1-2km)
    - Suburban: medium radius (3-5km)
    - Rural: larger radius (5-10km+)
    - Watch the circle expand on the map as you adjust
    - All users in this zone receive notifications
  </Step>

  <Step title="View on Public Map">
    Your lost pet appears on the community map:
    - Pin shows pet species icon and photo thumbnail
    - Click to view full report details
    - Search radius circle shows coverage area
    - Color coding indicates how recently posted (red = within 24hrs)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Sightings">
    As people report sightings:
    - New pins appear on your report's map
    - Lines connect sightings chronologically
    - Time stamps show when each sighting occurred
    - Direction and movement patterns emerge
    - Update your search focus based on patterns
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Expanding Search**: The platform can automatically expand your search radius by 50% after 48 hours if your pet isn't found. This ensures wider coverage as pets may travel farther over time.
</Tip>

## For Found Pets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mark Exact Found Location">
    When reporting a found pet:
    - Use "Current Location" if you're still at the spot
    - Or search and pin the precise location
    - Add landmarks visible in the area
    - Note if the pet was moving and in which direction
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Nearby Lost Reports">
    The map automatically shows:
    - Active lost pet reports within 5km
    - Visual comparison of found location vs. lost locations
    - How many days since each pet was reported lost
    - Direct links to potentially matching reports
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Match Alerts">
    The system compares locations:
    - Lost pet owners within radius get notified
    - Your found report appears on their map
    - Distance calculation shows proximity
    - Both parties can message through the platform
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sighting Reports

### For the Community

Spotted a lost pet? Add a sighting:

1. Find the lost pet report on the map or search
2. Click "Report Sighting"
3. Drop a pin where you saw the pet
4. Add date/time and any relevant details
5. Optionally upload a photo if you captured one
6. The owner receives immediate notification

<Info>
  **Sighting Impact**: Sighting reports increase successful reunions by 45%. Even if you can't capture the pet, reporting where you saw them helps owners focus their search.
</Info>

### Sighting Map Features

**On Your Lost Pet Report:**
- Original "last seen" location marked prominently
- Numbered sighting pins in chronological order
- Lines connecting sightings show travel path
- Hover over pins to see details and time
- Click pins to contact the person who reported the sighting

**Movement Analysis:**
- The map highlights likely search areas
- Algorithm suggests where to focus based on patterns
- Shows if pet is moving in a consistent direction
- Identifies potential "home base" if pet returns to areas

## Map Privacy Settings

<Warning>
  **Protect Your Privacy**: You control how precise your location appears to others. Consider your safety and privacy preferences when setting visibility.
</Warning>

### Location Visibility Options

**For Lost Pets:**
- **Exact Location**: Pin shows precise GPS coordinates (recommended for effective searching)
- **Approximate Area**: Pin shows general neighborhood (within 500m)
- **Hide from Public Map**: Report only visible to matches and direct shares

**For Found Pets:**
- **Exact Location**: Shows where pet was found (helps verify matches)
- **General Area**: Shows neighborhood only
- **Current Location**: Optional separate field for where pet is being kept (can hide until ownership verified)

**For Adoption Listings:**
- **Neighborhood Only**: Shows general area, exact address shared after approval
- **City Level**: Only shows Mar del Plata, specific location in messages
- **Custom Radius**: Show location within X meters

### Your Home Address Protection

- Never display your exact home address publicly without consent
- Use messaging to share precise pickup locations
- Meet-and-greet locations can be parks or public spaces
- Home visits require mutual agreement and verification

## Location-Based Notifications

### How Proximity Alerts Work

**You Receive Alerts When:**
- A pet is reported lost within your notification radius
- A pet is found near where you reported a lost pet
- Someone reports a sighting in your search area
- An adoptable pet matching your criteria is listed nearby

**Notification Radius Settings:**
- Default: 5km from your saved location(s)
- Customizable: 1km to 25km
- Multiple locations: home, work, frequent areas
- Temporary locations: when traveling

**Alert Frequency:**
- Immediate: real-time push notifications
- Daily digest: once per day summary
- Weekly summary: for lower urgency
- Pause temporarily when needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How accurate is the location tracking?">
  Location accuracy depends on the method used:
  - **GPS on mobile**: Accurate within 5-10 meters
  - **Address search**: Pinpoints specific address
  - **Dropped pin**: Exact spot selected on map
  - **IP address**: General area only (not recommended for reports)
  
  For best results, use GPS on mobile when at the actual location, or carefully search and pin the exact address.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I search multiple areas simultaneously?">
  Yes! You can:
  - Save multiple locations in your profile (home, work, family members' homes)
  - Receive notifications for all saved locations
  - Switch between locations using the map
  - Set different notification radii for each location
  
  This is helpful if your pet went missing while visiting somewhere, or if you want to help find pets in multiple neighborhoods.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I don't know the exact location?">
  If you're unsure of the precise spot:
  - Search for a nearby landmark or intersection
  - Drop a pin in the general area
  - In the description, note "approximate location" and provide details
  - You can always edit and update the location later
  - Add a wider search radius to compensate for uncertainty
  
  Approximate location is better than no location - it still helps the community narrow down the search area.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does the map work in all of Mar del Plata?">
  Yes, full Google Maps coverage includes:
  - All neighborhoods and streets in Mar del Plata
  - Surrounding areas and suburbs
  - Parks, beaches, and public spaces
  - Satellite view for detailed geography
  
  However, some very remote areas may have limited satellite imagery detail. The map functionality works everywhere, but imagery quality varies.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I export or share map data?">
  Yes, you can:
  - Share a specific report's map view via link
  - Generate a poster with QR code linking to your map
  - Export sighting coordinates to a file
  - Screenshot the map for social media sharing
  - Send map view to helpers coordinating the search
  
  Use the "Share" button on any map view to access these options.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How often does the map update?">
  The map updates in real-time:
  - New reports appear within seconds
  - Sighting additions show immediately
  - Status changes (found, adopted) reflected instantly
  - Layer filters apply immediately when toggled
  
  If you're not seeing expected updates, try refreshing the page or checking your filter settings.
</Accordion>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Verify Before Traveling**: Before driving to a sighting location or meet-and-greet, verify through platform messaging that the pet is still in the area and the meeting is confirmed.
</Tip>

### For Lost Pet Searches

1. **Start with last known location** and search outward in expanding circles
2. **Check the sighting map frequently** to adjust your search direction
3. **Use satellite view** to identify hiding spots like garages, sheds, or dense vegetation
4. **Coordinate with others** by sharing your map view and dividing search zones
5. **Report your own sightings** as you search to track covered areas

### For Community Helpers

1. **Enable notifications** for your area to help quickly
2. **Check the map daily** to see active cases nearby
3. **Report any pet sightings** even if you're not sure it matches
4. **Share reports** from your neighborhood on social media
5. **Offer to help search** organized areas on the map
