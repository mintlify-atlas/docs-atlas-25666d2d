## Setting Up Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Channels">
    Navigate to Settings → Notifications:
    - **WhatsApp**: Click "Connect WhatsApp" and scan QR code
    - **Email**: Verify your email address (sent during signup)
    - **Push**: Allow browser/app permissions when prompted
    - **SMS**: Add and verify your phone number (optional)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Notification Types">
    Select which events trigger alerts:
    - Lost pet reports in your area
    - Found pet matches for your reports
    - Adoption application status updates
    - Sighting reports for your lost pet
    - Messages and comments
    - Community activity in your neighborhoods
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Preferences">
    Customize delivery for each notification type:
    - Immediate vs. batched delivery
    - Which channels to use (mix and match)
    - Quiet hours (no notifications during sleep)
    - Location radius for proximity alerts
    - Frequency limits to avoid overwhelm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Priority Levels">
    Different urgency levels use different channels:
    - **Critical**: All enabled channels immediately
    - **High**: WhatsApp + Push immediately
    - **Medium**: Push + email within 1 hour
    - **Low**: Daily digest or in-app only
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Recommended Setup**: Enable WhatsApp for critical matches, email for daily digests, and push notifications for messages. This balance keeps you informed without overwhelming you.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why am I not receiving any notifications?">
  Check these common issues:
  
  1. **Permissions**: Verify browser/app notification permissions are enabled
  2. **Channels**: Ensure at least one channel (WhatsApp, email, push) is connected
  3. **Settings**: Check you haven't disabled specific notification types
  4. **Quiet Hours**: Verify you're not in Do Not Disturb mode
  5. **Location**: Confirm your location radius includes active reports
  6. **Verification**: Email must be verified to receive any notifications
  7. **Spam Folder**: Check if emails are going to spam/promotions
  8. **WhatsApp**: Ensure the business account isn't blocked
  
  Still not working? Contact support with your notification settings screenshot.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get notifications for a friend's lost pet?">
  Yes! There are several ways:
  
  1. **Follow a Report**: Open their lost pet report and click "Follow". You'll receive all updates as if it were your own pet.
  2. **Temporary Location**: Add their area as a temporary location in your settings
  3. **Share Account**: They can add you as a trusted helper with notification access
  4. **Manual Checks**: Bookmark their report and check regularly
  
  Following a report is the easiest method and gives you full visibility into matches and sightings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reduce notification spam without missing important alerts?">
  Try these strategies:
  
  1. **Use tiered channels**: WhatsApp for critical only, email for digests
  2. **Increase match threshold**: Only notify for 80%+ confidence matches
  3. **Narrow location radius**: Reduce from 5km to 2km if you're getting too many
  4. **Enable smart batching**: Group similar notifications together
  5. **Customize by time**: More alerts during active search hours, digest at other times
  6. **Disable community activity**: Focus only on direct matches and messages
  7. **Use focus mode**: Temporarily filter out everything except critical
  
  Never disable notifications completely - adjust them to your needs instead.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to notifications when I mark my pet as found?">
  When you mark your lost pet as found:
  
  - You stop receiving match and sighting notifications for that pet
  - The report is removed from public notification pools
  - Users who followed your report get a "reunited" notification
  - Your notification settings remain active for any other reports
  - You can still receive messages about that report for 30 days
  - All historical notifications remain in your archive
  
  Your general notification settings are unaffected.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I set up notifications for someone who isn't tech-savvy?">
  Yes, you can help someone else set up their notifications:
  
  1. **Use their email**: Set up their account with their email
  2. **Your WhatsApp**: Connect your WhatsApp to their account (you get their alerts)
  3. **SMS option**: Set up SMS to their phone (simpler than app)
  4. **Phone calls**: For extremely urgent matches, we can enable phone call alerts
  5. **Shared access**: Add yourself as a helper with notification permissions
  
  Recommended: Use their email for account, but your WhatsApp for immediate alerts. Forward important notifications to them via your preferred method.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there notification limits or costs?">
  Notification delivery is included free with Mascotas:
  
  - **Unlimited**: No cap on notification quantity
  - **Free**: All channels are free (WhatsApp, email, SMS, push)
  - **Fair Use**: Extremely high volume may trigger verification (anti-spam)
  - **SMS**: Limited to critical alerts only (to manage delivery costs)
  - **No ads**: Notifications never contain advertising
  
  We may introduce premium features in the future, but core notifications will always be free.
</Accordion>

## Notification Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Two-Channel Strategy**: Use WhatsApp for urgent, time-sensitive alerts and email for detailed digests. This combination ensures you never miss critical matches while keeping your inbox manageable.
</Tip>

### For Best Results:

1. **Verify all contact methods** during initial setup
2. **Test notifications** by following a sample report
3. **Adjust within 48 hours** based on actual volume
4. **Enable location services** for accurate proximity alerts
5. **Check notification center daily** even if you got push alerts
6. **Update preferences** as your situation changes (found pet, moved, etc.)
7. **Don't mute entirely** - use quiet hours and frequency controls instead

### Response Expectations:

- **Critical match**: Respond within 1 hour if possible
- **Sighting report**: Acknowledge within 2-4 hours
- **Messages**: Reply within 24 hours
- **Adoption applications**: Review within 48 hours
- **Community alerts**: No response required

Quick responses increase the likelihood of successful reunions and adoptions. The notification system is designed to make responding fast and easy.
